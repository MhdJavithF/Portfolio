A bit about me

I'm a Frontend Engineer who loves turning complex requirements into fast, clean, and accessible digital experiences. I build production-grade applications using React.js, Next.js, and TypeScript — with a strong eye on performance, scalability, and maintainability.

At Accenture, I work on a large-scale UK telecom web platform — integrating Contentstack CMS, optimising Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS), running Optimizely A/B experiments, and managing releases through Concourse CI and Octopus Deploy.
